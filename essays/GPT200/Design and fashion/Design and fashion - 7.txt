I often wonder if the pressure to stay relevant has clouded my perspective on personal fulfillment. The constant comparison with younger models can be suffocating, leaving me feeling like I’m a relic rather than someone who has contributed to the industry. Yet, through this anxiety, I’ve started to explore what truly ignites my passion beyond the catwalk. Perhaps creative direction or photography could offer me a renewed sense of purpose. Learning how to express beauty through a different lens fascinates me; after all, there’s a wealth of experience to draw from, and my insights could guide emerging talent. Transitioning from being the subject to nurturing others feels like a natural evolution, albeit one steeped in uncertainty. Friends have suggested I leverage my connections and use my story as a springboard into the fashion industry behind the scenes. Every time I think about the next step, it becomes crystal clear that I don't want to fade into obscurity without leaving a meaningful mark. It's about reclaiming my narrative—not just as a model, but as a storyteller who can shape the perceptions of beauty and inspire the next generation. The journey of self-discovery is daunting, yet it’s one I’m eager to embark upon.
