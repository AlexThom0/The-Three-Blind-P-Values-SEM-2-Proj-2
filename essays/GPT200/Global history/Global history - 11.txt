This culinary evolution reflects broader economic shifts, serving as a microcosm of the changes wrought by global trade dynamics and colonial exploitation. Understanding these shifts invites a deeper appreciation of how history continually shapes cultural narratives. The intersection of nutrition, tradition, and socio-economic reality in the Gambia underscores the complex layers of historical reinvention that reflect contemporary challenges.
