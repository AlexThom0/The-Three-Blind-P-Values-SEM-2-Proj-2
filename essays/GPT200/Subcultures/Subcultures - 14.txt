These young gamers often immerse themselves in virtual worlds, where the lines between reality and fantasy blur, creating an environment that is difficult to escape. For many, the thrill of virtual victory can be far more compelling than the challenges of real life. Tao Hongkai employs various strategies during his sessions, aiming to cultivate a healthier relationship with technology. He emphasizes self-reflection, encouraging these youths to acknowledge the emotional triggers behind their gaming. Through dialogue and engagement, he challenges them to consider what they value most. By sharing personal stories, he fosters a sense of community among the participants, allowing them to recognize that they are not alone in their struggles. This social aspect often motivates them to commit to positive change. Rather than merely admonishing their habits, Tao helps them forge new paths, highlighting the potential for personal growth that can emerge from overcoming addiction to online distractions. The journey to reclaim their lives, while difficult, can ultimately lead to newfound resilience and purpose.
