Each new name expanded her stylistic repertoire and underscored the importance of perspective in philosophy, much like Pessoa's heteronyms, which embodied distinct worldviews and literary approaches. Thus, pseudonymous writing serves not merely as a tool for anonymity but transforms into an active engagement with the narratives and beliefs of diverse cultures. In doing so, it fosters a multifaceted discourse that challenges conventional boundaries within academic and literary landscapes.
