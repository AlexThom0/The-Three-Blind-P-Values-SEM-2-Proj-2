The fascination with these images did not merely entertain; it sparked curiosity among scientists and laypeople alike, propelling the advancement of microscopy into a serious scientific discipline. The impact of this work demonstrated that a humble instrument could unlock profound insights, fundamentally altering how life sciences were approached.
