Each artifact I preserve tells a tale of resilience and adaptation, much like my own journey through life. The high heels may symbolize my playful aspirations, but they coexist with the heavy burden of memory carried from generation to generation. The artifacts act as a compass, guiding my son through the rich narratives that have shaped our lineage and imparting invaluable lessons about connection and belonging. As I sift through my collection, I find that reclaiming these artifacts doesn’t just honor those who came before me; it also cultivates a sense of purpose within myself, transforming what could feel like weight into an emblem of strength. Every item collected holds the promise of a story yet to be told, resonating with the legacy I hope to pass on.
