Keynote speeches filled with inspiring rhetoric clashed with the surrounding atmosphere of surveillance and control, leaving many to ponder whether true innovation could thrive within such constraints. As participants navigated these complex layers of influence, it became clear that redefining the relationship between passion and profit was essential for preserving the spirit of radical thought.
