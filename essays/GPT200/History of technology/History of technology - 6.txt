The integration of psychology into military strategy rapidly expanded, influencing policies and training programs within the armed forces. In a climate where trust in institutions was wavering, these psychological tactics aimed not merely at winning battles but at winning hearts and minds, paving the way for a new era of conflict that transcended traditional weaponry.
