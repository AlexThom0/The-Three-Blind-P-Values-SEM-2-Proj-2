The allure of imperial service promised wealth, yet it also required a sacrifice of individuality, compelling these young Romans to prioritize the collective values of their institutions over their personal aspirations. The dynamic created a complex relationship between ambition and conformity in a world where one’s success was intricately tied to their ability to adhere to the prevailing order.
