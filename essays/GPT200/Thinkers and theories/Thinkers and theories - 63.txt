By recognizing the inherent worth of every member, regardless of their social standing, she argues that societies can cultivate a more cohesive and compassionate environment. This requires a shift in perception that nurtures empathy and understanding, fostering connections among diverse groups. Establishing a framework where all voices contribute to the dialogue not only strengthens communal ties but also ensures that marginalized perspectives are acknowledged. Only by embracing this holistic approach can societies hope to address the complexities of human existence and build a more just and equitable world.
