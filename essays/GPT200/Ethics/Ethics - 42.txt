The emotional turmoil that accompanies such changes requires more than just a binary reaction; it invites us to engage deeply with the underlying principles and beliefs that inform our current norms. This examination is not merely academic but vital for our collective well-being. Reflection on our values invites a dialogue about how we adapt—or resist—these transformations.
