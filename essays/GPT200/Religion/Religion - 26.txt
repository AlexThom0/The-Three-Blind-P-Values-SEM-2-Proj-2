Such experiences are central to the practitioners' identities, painted vividly through personal testimonies that explore how these roles foster emotional and spiritual growth within their marriages. These deeply personal exchanges significantly enrich their relationships, creating a framework that allows for both individual fulfillment and mutual satisfaction. The emotional landscape of CDD is, therefore, vibrant and multifaceted, challenging reductive interpretations of power and submission.
