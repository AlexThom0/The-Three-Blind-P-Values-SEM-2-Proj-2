In this view, the author’s overtly imaginative framework provides a unique environment where the suspension of disbelief is not merely invited but actively constructed. Readers engage with fictional narratives in a way that allows them to explore complex moral dilemmas, emotional landscapes, and imaginative worlds without the burden of real-world consequences. This dynamic sets fiction apart from both lying and irony, as the interactivity relies on a mutual understanding of the artifice involved. Here, the truth derives not from correspondences to reality but from the internal coherence of the story itself, where characters and events, while fabricated, resonate with deeper truths about the human experience. The richness of this engagement invites interpretations and emotional responses that go beyond mere observation, asking readers to inhabit the perspectives of characters and to grapple with the implications of their journeys. Understanding this immersive quality is crucial to appreciating the distinction between fiction and other forms of discourse that invoke falsehoods, as it positions fiction as a meaningful exploration rather than a mere deceptive act.
