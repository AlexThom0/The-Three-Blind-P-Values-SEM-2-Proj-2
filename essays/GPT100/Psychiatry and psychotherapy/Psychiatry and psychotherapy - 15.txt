In the face of rising anxiety and depression among youth, the therapeutic landscape tends to prioritize quick fixes over sustained emotional engagement. This shift not only undermines the potential for genuine personal growth but also devalues the critical importance of therapeutic relationships. Children today might lack the enduring support that can help them navigate inner turmoil, leaving them more isolated.
