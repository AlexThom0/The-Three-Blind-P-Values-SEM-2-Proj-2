Participants showed varying levels of competency, with some performing adequately even under the influence of cannabis. This complexity underscored that not all intoxication effects are created equal; while marijuana can impair reaction times and attention, the resultant driving impairments may differ significantly from those caused by alcohol. Over time, this perspective shifted public discourse, leading many to question the absolute danger attributed to cannabis use while driving. Understanding these variations is crucial in forming balanced policies.
