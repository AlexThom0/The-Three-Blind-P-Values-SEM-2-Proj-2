The social stigma surrounding imperfect teeth creates a cycle of shame and neglect, pushing individuals deeper into the financial and emotional abyss. For many, appointments are avoided not just due to cost, but also the anxiety associated with being judged by dental professionals. The frustration grows when one realizes that preventive care is rarely accessible, transforming what could have been routine check-ups into catastrophic waits for emergency treatments. Teeth, often termed a mere accessory, become emblematic of larger systemic failures that leave entire communities grappling with the consequences of neglectful policies.
