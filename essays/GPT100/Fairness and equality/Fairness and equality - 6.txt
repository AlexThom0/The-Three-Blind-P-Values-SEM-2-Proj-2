The cultural impact of Hamer’s findings combined with the societal struggles faced by the LGBTQ+ community began to shift perceptions worldwide. Advocates embraced the narrative of biological determinism, viewing it as a shield against stigma and discrimination. Media representations, like Gaga’s anthem, became a powerful tool for visibility, fostering acceptance and inclusivity. However, this focus on genetics risked oversimplifying the complexities of sexual orientation, ignoring the interplay of environmental influences, personal experiences, and social factors. It established a dichotomy that could undermine the richness of individual narratives, reducing diverse experiences to a single, flawed concept of identity determined purely by biology.
