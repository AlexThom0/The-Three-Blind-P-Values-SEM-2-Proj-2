This transition marked a pivotal moment when the subjective qualities of sound became secondary to objective analysis. Music, once intertwined with daily life and emotion, started to resemble a mathematical equation, delineating a clear division between the intuitive practices of artists and the empirical pursuits of scholars. This redefined relationship profoundly influenced cultural perceptions of music, privileging those who could dissect sound into its components while sidelining the raw, unmeasured beauty that had initially inspired human connection.
