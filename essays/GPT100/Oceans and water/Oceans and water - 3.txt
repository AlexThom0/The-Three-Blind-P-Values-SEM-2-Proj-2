The complex interactions among species and their environments are deeply interconnected, and the loss of these habitats not only jeopardizes individual fish populations but also disrupts food webs. As predator and prey dynamics shift, species that rely on the coral and sponge structures for shelter and breeding are increasingly vulnerable.
