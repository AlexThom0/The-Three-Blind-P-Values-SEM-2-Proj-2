Chester called for a reclamation of narrative, urging communities to challenge the dominant imagery that reinforced racial hierarchies. By demanding fair representation, individuals could assert their dignity and reshape discourse around identity. Each act of resistance was a step toward a more inclusive understanding of humanity.
