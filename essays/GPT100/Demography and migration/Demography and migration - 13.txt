Many young professionals view foreign experiences as essential to their careers, often believing that skills acquired abroad will not only enhance their employability but also shield them from local instability. This prospect of leaving is further fueled by narratives of success and safety that circulate among social media circles. Entrepreneurs and tech start-ups sprout up in urban centers, yet their founders often harbor plans to expand or relocate internationally as soon as they achieve a stable footing. The allure of the West grows with each passing year, undermining any sense of commitment to the homeland.
