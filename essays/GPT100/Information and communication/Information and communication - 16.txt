Those affected, often marginalized communities, may find themselves subject to decisions made by algorithms they cannot understand or contest. It is crucial to foster a culture of transparency where stakeholders can scrutinize and challenge the use of these technologies. By advocating for ethical standards and inclusive practices, we can work toward mitigating harm and ensuring that technological advancements serve rather than undermine social justice. The path forward lies in collaboration between technologists, policymakers, and affected communities.
