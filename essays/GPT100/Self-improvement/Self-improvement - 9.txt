By presenting exaggerated circumstances and emotional extremes, it captures the essence of human vulnerability, eliciting visceral responses that resonate deeply. Viewers, much like those of ancient tragedies, find themselves grappling with their own empathy and disgust. This engagement not only hooks audiences but also prompts reflection on moral dilemmas and social realities, ultimately transforming our understanding of both the spectacle and ourselves. The interplay of spectacle and real emotion creates a unique type of catharsis.
