Yet, amidst our fast-paced lives, distractions abound, making sustained attention seem a daunting task. Technology often exacerbates this issue; devices regularly interrupt interactions, pulling us away from meaningful exchanges. The art of listening, therefore, must be reclaimed as a deliberate practice, one that necessitates our full presence. Only by prioritizing this fundamental skill can we foster genuine understanding and compassion in our relationships. True listening requires effort, but the rewards are profound.
