These structures not only symbolize the shift from agrarian roots to industrial behemoths but also reflect the systematic prioritization of efficiency and profit over community and local economies. As wealth and power consolidated, the needs of ordinary citizens were increasingly eclipsed by the ambitions of corporate giants, leading to an era where political discourse became disconnected from the everyday realities faced by the populace. This disjunction created a fertile ground for populist movements, as disenfranchised voices sought to reclaim agency in a rapidly changing world.
