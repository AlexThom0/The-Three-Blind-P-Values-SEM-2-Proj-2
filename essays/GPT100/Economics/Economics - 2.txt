The lack of transparency in these digital asset markets not only invites deception but also perpetuates a culture of distrust among investors. As speculative bubbles inflate, driven by hype and misinformation, the inherent volatility becomes a breeding ground for unethical practices. This situation is exacerbated by the ease with which false narratives can be disseminated online, making it increasingly difficult for investors to distinguish between genuine opportunities and elaborate hoaxes. The rapid evolution of technology creates an arms race between scammers and regulators, highlighting the urgent need for a comprehensive framework that prioritizes transparency and accountability in these burgeoning markets.
