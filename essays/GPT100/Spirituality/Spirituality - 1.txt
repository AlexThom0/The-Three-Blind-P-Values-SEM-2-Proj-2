The allure of Varanasi lies not just in its ancient rituals or its breathtaking ghats; it persists as a symbol of resilience, a place where the old and new intertwine harmoniously. Here, time seems to stand still, yet the city's relentless pace pulls in both the lost and the seekers. For many, the chaotic yet vibrant atmosphere becomes a backdrop for self-discovery, prompting questions about identity and belonging. This magnetic pull is compelling, inviting individuals like Peter and Shyam Baba to immerse themselves in its depths, whether for a brief visit or a new life altogether.
