By utilizing the unique environmental conditions, telescopes on the Moon will enable unprecedented discoveries about exoplanets, their atmospheres, and potential biosignatures. The Moon acts as a perfect vantage point, offering advantages such as reduced light pollution and minimal interference from Earth’s own atmospheric distortions. As these instruments gather data, they will not only enhance our understanding of the cosmic landscape but also reshape our perspective on humanity’s place within it, fostering a deeper connection to the universe that surrounds us.
