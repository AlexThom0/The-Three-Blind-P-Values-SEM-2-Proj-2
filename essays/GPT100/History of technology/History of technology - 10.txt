This approach not only captured the horse's motion but also laid the groundwork for the principles of motion picture technology. Muybridge meticulously arranged the photographs to reveal sequential movements, providing a comprehensive analysis of the horse’s gait. His experiments garnered significant attention and sparked debates about the nature of motion and reality in art.
