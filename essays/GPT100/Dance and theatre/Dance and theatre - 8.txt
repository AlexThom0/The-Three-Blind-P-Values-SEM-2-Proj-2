The concept resonated deeply within the local creative community, as artists often faced both financial constraints and a lack of access to essential services. Cingel’s initiative inspired a collective brainstorming session among these creative individuals, leading them to explore how they could further enhance the exchange between artistry and healthcare. They envisioned a platform where musicians could not only perform but also share their stories about the impact of healthcare on their lives and careers. This synergy between art and well-being forged a unique connection that captured the attention of both local and national audiences.
