Many of these leaders emerge with a superficial understanding of what it means to lead, often lacking the practical skills necessary for real-world challenges. This disconnect between theory and practice reinforces a cycle where the true essence of leadership gets diluted into trendy concepts and buzzwords. This phenomenon highlights a growing trend of misaligned priorities within corporate training initiatives.
