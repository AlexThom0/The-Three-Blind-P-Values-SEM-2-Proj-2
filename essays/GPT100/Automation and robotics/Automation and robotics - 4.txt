Cryptocurrencies were initially viewed as a potential solution, promising lower costs and decentralized operations. However, the volatility of these digital assets, along with their often opaque governance structures, has led many to question their reliability. As users and companies are drawn into a system marked by speculation rather than genuine exchange, the promise of a truly collaborative economic model slips further away. Ultimately, without viable alternatives that foster genuine community trust, the vision of the Collaborative Commons remains just that: a vision.
