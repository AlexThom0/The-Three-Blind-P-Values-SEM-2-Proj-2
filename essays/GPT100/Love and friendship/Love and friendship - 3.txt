For men, the fear of sexual infidelity stems not only from a desire for genetic continuity but also from the concern of paternal investment; losing a partner to another man means losing both emotional and financial support. Similarly, women gauge the emotional fidelity of their partners as a critical measure of their investment in the relationship. The palpable anxiety surrounding these dynamics helps to explain the primal basis of jealousy, raising questions about trust, loyalty, and the complex interplay of human relationships.
