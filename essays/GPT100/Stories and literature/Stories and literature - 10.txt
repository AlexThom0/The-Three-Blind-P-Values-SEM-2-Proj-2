However, I soon realized that this approach could inadvertently foster dependency rather than independence. Children might interpret relentless warmth as a condition for their worth, leading them to believe that expressions of dissent or frustration were unacceptable. My efforts, while well-meaning, seemed to blur the lines of boundaries and self-expression, making it difficult for my children to navigate their own emotions. This realization prompted a critical shift in my parenting strategy, reinforcing the need for balance in our interactions to nurture their individuality.
