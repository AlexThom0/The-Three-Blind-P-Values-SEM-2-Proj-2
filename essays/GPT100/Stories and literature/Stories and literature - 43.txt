The characters, while steeped in the supernatural, encapsulate a deep-seated yearning for connection to a past that offered clarity and purpose. As urban life became increasingly regimented and rationalized, these ghostly encounters provided a necessary lens through which the populace could navigate their shifting realities. The stories became vessels of cultural memory, capturing not merely fears of the unknown, but also a longing for the intimate ties once held with nature and the unseen forces that had governed life.
