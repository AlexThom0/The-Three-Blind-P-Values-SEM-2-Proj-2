Consequently, the genetic landscape of modern populations is a tapestry woven with threads of ancient lineages and migrations. Each region’s genetic profile not only reflects its inhabitants’ current health and traits but also serves as a record of their past—depicting how environmental pressures and cultural exchanges have influenced genetic selection. Understanding this complex interplay deepens our insight into human evolution, revealing the intricate legacy of our shared ancestry.
