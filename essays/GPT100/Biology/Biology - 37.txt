Europa, with its subsurface ocean heated by tidal forces, presents an environment where the essential ingredients for life—water, energy, and organic molecules—could coexist. The allure of these worlds has spurred innovative technologies and missions aimed at peering beneath their surfaces, suggesting that the quest for extraterrestrial life may find its most fruitful paths in these distant, yet tantalizingly close, celestial realms.
