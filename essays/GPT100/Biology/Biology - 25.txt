Historical examples abound where paradigms have shifted drastically, challenging long-held beliefs. Consider the transition from geocentrism to heliocentrism, which wasn't merely a refinement of existing knowledge but a complete overhaul of how humanity positioned itself within the cosmos. Such upheavals often lead to friction among practitioners and the public. This resistance highlights the human tendency to cling to established views, revealing that our relationship with knowledge involves both an intellectual and emotional journey. Embracing this reality is essential for genuine scientific progress.
