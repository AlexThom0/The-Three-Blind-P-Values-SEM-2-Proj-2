For instance, if bees can engage in offline thinking, what implications does this have for their social interactions and decision-making? Bees navigate complex environments, making choices that reflect not just immediate needs but also future conditions. This foresight suggests a level of cognitive sophistication previously attributed only to larger-brained animals.
