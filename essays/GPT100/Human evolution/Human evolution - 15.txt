Each newly identified species not only captures the imagination but also reinforces the narrative of human history as a complex tapestry of interwoven threads. This obsession with naming often leads to confusion, as multiple classifications may stem from slight variations that could simply reflect natural adaptability rather than distinct evolutionary lines.
