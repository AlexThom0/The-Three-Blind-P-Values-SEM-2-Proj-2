Through this interplay, Newbery illustrates how seemingly trivial tools can possess significant cultural and educational value. The pin, an item often overlooked, transforms into a pedagogical instrument, symbolizing how familiar objects can engage young minds. This whimsical anthropomorphism not only entertains but also reinforces the notion that learning can be enriched through creative approaches.
