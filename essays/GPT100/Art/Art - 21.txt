By placing Peter Martyr within the scene, Angelico deepens the connection between the sacred and the contemporary experience of the friars. This interaction suggests a continuity of faith that unites past and present, allowing the viewer to reflect on their own spiritual journey in light of the divine conversation unfolding before them. Such layers of meaning enrich the meditation that takes place in the friar's cell, transforming a static image into a dynamic source of inspiration and contemplation for those who gaze upon it.
