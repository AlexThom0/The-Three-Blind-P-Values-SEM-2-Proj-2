Cognitive flexibility allows individuals to reassess their situation and re-evaluate potential outcomes based on new information. It empowers one to pivot from a rigid trajectory when faced with obstacles, thereby increasing the likelihood of finding viable alternatives. This is not merely a survival mechanism but an essential component of thriving in complex environments. Without this variability in thought and action, an individual risks becoming trapped in a loop of ineffective choices, reinforcing the analogy with the automaton whose fixed patterns ultimately fail to yield satisfactory results.
