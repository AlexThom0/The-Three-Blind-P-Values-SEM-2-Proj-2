They may anthropomorphize them, assigning names and emotions, yet face the harsh realities of animal husbandry that challenge these romantic notions. This duality reveals conflicting attitudes toward animal welfare; while the affection for brooders exists, the lack of understanding about their needs, behaviors, and biological realities can lead to unintentional neglect.
