This widening gap highlighted an urgent need for a reassessment of the role of humanities in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Scholars began to argue that instead of succumbing to irrelevance, the humanities could provide critical insights into ethical implications and human values that were increasingly overshadowed by scientific advancements.
