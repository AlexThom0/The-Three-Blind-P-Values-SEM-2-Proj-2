This surge in public mobilization is often driven by the advent of social media, which facilitates rapid organization and dissemination of grievances. People increasingly feel empowered to demand accountability and transparency from their governments, irrespective of previous patterns of dissent. Moreover, economic disparities and perceived injustices cannot be overlooked; these issues resonate deeply with marginalized communities, fueling a collective sense of urgency.
