The conflicts, while often framed in ideological terms, derived from deeper, more persistent human grievances and power struggles. Gray’s insistence on the perils of misplaced faith in progress resonates powerfully in these contexts, where the very constructs of civilization can unravel in the face of primal instincts and social fractures.
