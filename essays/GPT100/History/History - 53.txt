This shift marked a departure from the esoteric traditions that had long dominated the discipline, instead inviting practitioners of all backgrounds to engage with alchemical principles in tangible ways. The recipes not only opened doors to practical skills but also democratized knowledge that had previously been reserved for an elite few. By demonstrating the applicability of alchemical techniques in common settings, Egenolff positioned alchemy as an essential craft, integral to both domestic and industrial spheres, fostering innovation and enhancing livelihoods.
