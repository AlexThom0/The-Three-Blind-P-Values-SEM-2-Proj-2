The focus is on efficiency, profit maximization, and scalability, sidelining the imperative to innovate responsibly. Consequently, the educational framework promotes a narrow view of success where traditional metrics overshadow important social and environmental values. Ultimately, the imposition of these market-driven ideologies stifles the creativity required to envision and implement genuinely sustainable futures.
