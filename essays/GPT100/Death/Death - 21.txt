The rituals surrounding death can shape our understanding and acceptance of it, serving to demystify the process. In cultures where open acknowledgment of death is common, there may be less fear and more reverence for the cycle of existence. Embracing the reality of mortality can foster a deeper appreciation for life itself, allowing individuals to engage with their own stories and those of their loved ones more fully. Each moment becomes precious when viewed through the lens of impermanence.
