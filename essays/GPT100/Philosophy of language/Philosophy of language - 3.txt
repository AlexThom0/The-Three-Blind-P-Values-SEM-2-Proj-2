This engagement is not merely a passive reception; it involves an active interpretation where the listener identifies and acknowledges the speaker's intent. The relationship between speaker and audience is therefore crucial, as it shapes the communicative landscape. If this interplay is missing, the illocutionary act fails to achieve its purpose, highlighting a deeper issue about the social structures that support or hinder these interactions.
