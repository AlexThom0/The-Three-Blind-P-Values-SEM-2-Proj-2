The simplistic equation of calories in versus calories out now feels inadequate when juxtaposed with findings that highlight the complexities of energy regulation and hormonal responses. Additionally, psychosocial elements play a crucial role; cultural attitudes toward food and body image can significantly impact eating behaviors.
