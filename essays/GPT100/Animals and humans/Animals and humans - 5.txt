As poodles evolved from rugged working dogs to refined companions, their grooming and presentation reflect broader societal trends, particularly the move toward an idealized form of beauty influenced by fashion and status. This transformation has drawn attention to the ethics of selective breeding, raising concerns about the health implications for these dogs. The meticulous care required for their coats further complicates the relationship between owners and their pets, stretching beyond mere companionship to a commitment demanding time, resources, and knowledge.
