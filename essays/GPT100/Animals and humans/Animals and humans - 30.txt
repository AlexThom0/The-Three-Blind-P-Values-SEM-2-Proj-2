However, the implications of habituation must be carefully considered; when dolphins seek out humans, it raises questions about their instincts and well-being. The challenge remains balancing human desire for connection with the dolphins' need for autonomy, especially in environments where interactions can lead to dependency or altered behaviors. Such dynamics underscore the importance of maintaining clear boundaries to ensure both human safety and dolphin welfare.
