Each consultation left me more disheartened, reinforcing the isolation that many artists face when their identity is bound to their craft. The disconnect between my lived reality and the clinical perspective was stark; they saw a patient, while I felt like a ghost of my former self. Their sterile theories offered no solace, and the lack of empathy in their responses only deepened my despair.
