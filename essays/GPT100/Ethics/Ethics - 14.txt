As we navigate our daily decisions, there emerges an inherent recognition not only of our own goals but also of the framework within which we operate. This recognition fosters an awareness that our choices impact a wider network of beings, prompting a moral responsibility that extends beyond mere human interactions. Engaging with the interests of others becomes integral to our understanding of agency. By acknowledging that our decisions shape the experiences of those around us, we naturally cultivate a sense of empathy that urges us to consider not just our own desires, but also the legitimate aspirations of others, enriching our moral landscape.
