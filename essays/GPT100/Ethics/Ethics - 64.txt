As species decline and their habitats shrink, the natural balance is threatened, leading to cascading effects that can impact human populations as well. The connection between agricultural practices and wildlife health is stark; sustainable farming methods could mitigate some of these issues, allowing for a coexistence that benefits both agriculture and the surrounding ecosystems.
