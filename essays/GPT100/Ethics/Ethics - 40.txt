Witnessing such a stark contrast between the idyllic images of pastoral farming and the harsh realities of industrial agriculture deepened my curiosity about the origins of my food. It was unsettling, yet enlightening, to understand that these systems favored productivity over the artistry of traditional butchery. This disconnection from the source of our nourishment fostered a desire to explore the visceral experience of hunting and processing meat myself, prompting reflections on ethics, sustainability, and the complex relationship we cultivate with the animals we consume.
