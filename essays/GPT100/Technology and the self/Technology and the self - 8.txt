the intention behind Alisa’s design was to mirror the resilience ingrained in the cultural psyche. This resilience, shaped by centuries of hardship, demands a character that resonates with the audience's lived experiences. Alisa’s blend of straightforwardness and an underlying wit reflects a collective identity that often finds strength in coping mechanisms rather than in overt displays of kindness. This nuanced approach allows her to transcend mere functionality; she becomes a cultural artifact, embodying the complex interplay between humor and survival that has defined Russian narratives throughout history. The aim was to forge an ally, not just a companion.
