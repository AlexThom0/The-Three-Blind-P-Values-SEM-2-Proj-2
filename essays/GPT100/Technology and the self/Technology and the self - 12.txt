This affinity for the unadorned extends beyond mere nostalgia; it represents a conscious choice to embrace the tactile and the tangible in a world dominated by the transient and the virtual. Each object I find solace in holds a history, a story woven through time and experience, contrasting sharply with the facelessness of contemporary technology. The simplicity of these artifacts fosters a connection not just with the past but with the present, encouraging mindfulness in their use. They invite us to slow down and recognize the intrinsic value in things that require our engagement, rather than passive consumption and constant upgrades.
