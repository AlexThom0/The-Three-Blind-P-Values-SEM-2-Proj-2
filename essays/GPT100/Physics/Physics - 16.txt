They would meticulously examine the data collected, comparing it against theoretical predictions and previous experiments. Any anomalies in the readings could provide crucial insights into neutrino behavior and properties. Given that neutrinos hardly interact with matter, the challenge of detecting them posed significant obstacles, not only in experimental design but also in the interpretation of results. Successful detection could confirm or refute existing models, pushing the boundaries of particle physics.
