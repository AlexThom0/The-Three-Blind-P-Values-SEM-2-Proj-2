This phenomenon not only diminished the human spirit but also raised profound ethical questions about the consequences of commodifying existence. As technology advanced, it increasingly dictated values and priorities, leading to a disconnection from the moral considerations once inherent to our actions. The existential threat posed by this disconnect demanded urgent analysis, as it signified a collective retreat from compassion and introspection. In grappling with these issues, Anders sought to illuminate the urgent need for renewed awareness and responsibility amidst a rapidly changing landscape that often prioritized efficiency over humanity.
