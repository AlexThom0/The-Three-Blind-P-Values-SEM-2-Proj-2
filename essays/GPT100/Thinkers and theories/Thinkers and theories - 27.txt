For instance, when women's economic contributions remain unrecognized, the dominant narratives surrounding work and productivity become incomplete. This intersection of social reality and knowledge creation demands a reevaluation of the standards by which expertise is measured. Engaging with these diverse perspectives not only enriches academic discourse but also helps illuminate the often-hidden dynamics of power that influence whose voices are heard and valued in societal discussions.
