If humanity is deemed a pollutant by some, then the challenge lies in redefining our legacy, shifting the narrative from one of destruction to one of stewardship. The potential for creativity, empathy, and innovation within our species represents a unique opportunity to influence the ecological balance positively. To diminish our role entirely overlooks the remarkable achievements we have made in science, art, and social reform. Acknowledging our faults is essential, but so is recognizing our capacity for growth and healing.
