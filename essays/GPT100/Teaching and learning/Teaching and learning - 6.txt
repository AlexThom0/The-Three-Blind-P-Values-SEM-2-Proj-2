In this context, the classroom should serve as a dynamic space where students feel empowered to share their thoughts and challenge the status quo. Encouraging such an environment not only cultivates independent thinkers but also fosters a community of learners who respect diverse perspectives. By allowing students to engage in discussions that may seem uncomfortable, we begin to dismantle the superficial layers of knowledge acquired solely for testing purposes. This transformative approach can incite a lifelong passion for learning, urging students to seek out knowledge beyond classroom walls.
