The allure of masks extended beyond mere aesthetics; they became a symbol of empowerment for women navigating societal constraints. By adopting a mask, a woman simultaneously embraced anonymity and authority, granting her the freedom to transcend the limitations placed upon her. This duality fostered an environment where personal desires could be expressed without the traditional repercussions. In clandestine gatherings or theatrical performances, these masks not only concealed identity but also liberated women from the burdens of their social roles, transforming their interactions into a dance of intrigue and autonomy, ripe for exploration and self-expression.
