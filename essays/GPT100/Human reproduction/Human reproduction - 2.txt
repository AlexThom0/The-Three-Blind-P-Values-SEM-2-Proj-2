This portrayal extends beyond mere biological necessity; it reinforces the idea of motherhood as an identity steeped in sacrifice and selflessness. Such imagery not only romanticizes the act of childbirth but also perpetuates the notion that a woman's worth is maximized through her reproductive capabilities.
