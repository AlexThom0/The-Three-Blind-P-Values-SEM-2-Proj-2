This false perception creates a cycle of irrational betting, pushing individuals to chase losses or make riskier bets under the delusion that fortune is about to change. Moreover, the ways in which games are designed often accentuate this feeling, with visual and auditory cues that reinforce the illusion of winning potential. Such manipulative tactics keep players engaged, ultimately obscuring the true odds and leading to increasingly poor decision-making in their gambling activities.
