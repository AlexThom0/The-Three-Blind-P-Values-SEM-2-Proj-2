By blurring the lines between natural abilities and artificial enhancements, the current anti-doping framework risks undermining the very spirit of competition. Athletes today have access to advanced technologies that can enhance performance in ways previously unimaginable. Instead of clinging to outdated notions of purity, a more nuanced approach could recognize physiological enhancements as just another evolution in the pursuit of excellence. Embracing this perspective could pave the way for a more equitable and innovative sporting environment.
