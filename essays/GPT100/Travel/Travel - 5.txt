As trains traversed vast landscapes, they served as mobile microcosms, where individuals shared not just journeys but also experiences and conversations. Such encounters fostered a sense of belonging and highlighted the interplay between anonymity and connection that train travel engendered. This unique environment became a stage for the evolving role of women in public life, as they navigated newfound freedoms and the complexities of shared space.
