Social media platforms allow these communities to share their own stories, mobilize support, and advocate for their needs. This shift not only democratizes information but also fosters a sense of agency among affected populations. Consequently, NGOs are compelled to reconsider their roles; they can no longer act solely as intermediaries but must collaborate with local voices to ensure that relief efforts resonate with the realities on the ground. Such collaboration redefines the dynamics of aid, emphasizing partnership over paternalism.
