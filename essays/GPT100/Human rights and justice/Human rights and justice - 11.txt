Each link in the chain can introduce complexities that obscure accountability, with many companies relying on third-party suppliers who may not adhere to the same ethical standards. This obscurity creates a challenge not just for consumers, but also for regulatory bodies striving to enforce compliance. Furthermore, the rapid increase in e-commerce has exacerbated these issues, amplifying the demand for quick production cycles and, consequently, placing immense pressure on suppliers. Addressing these challenges requires a comprehensive approach that fosters transparency and enforces standards throughout the entire supply chain.
