This blind spot in the collective consciousness allows for a simplified portrayal of history where the triumph of civilization overshadows the profound loss experienced by indigenous populations. By framing the narrative around conquest and civilization, key elements of violence and resistance are either sanitized or forgotten entirely, stripping away the humanity of the victims.
