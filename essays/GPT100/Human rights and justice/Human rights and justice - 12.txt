This trend raises significant questions about accountability and oversight, as these entities often operate without the same checks and balances that govern public institutions. Furthermore, the privatization of these functions can create conflicts of interest, where profit motives may undermine justice and public safety. Such arrangements blur the lines between public responsibility and private profit, complicating the ethical landscape of governance.
