This multidisciplinary approach not only highlights the intricacies of agricultural practices but also reveals the social dynamics within herding communities. The evidence gathered provides insights into breeding choices driven by human preference, which significantly shaped the evolutionary trajectories of species. Additionally, these findings illuminate pathways of trade and migration, further intertwining the stories of people and the domesticated species they cultivated. Understanding these interactions is essential to grasping the broader impacts of domestication on human societies.
