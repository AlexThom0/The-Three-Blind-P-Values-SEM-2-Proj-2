Misinterpretations of archaeological sites further fueled the belief that sophisticated societies could not have originated with Native Americans. Instead, the achievements of Indigenous peoples were attributed to foreign influences, reinforcing a colonial mindset that dismissed the rich history and contributions of the First Peoples. Such distortions served to justify injustices while obscuring the true legacy of a diverse and complex pre-Columbian America.
