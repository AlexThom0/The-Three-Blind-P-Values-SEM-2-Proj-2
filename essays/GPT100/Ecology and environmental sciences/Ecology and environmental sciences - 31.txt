This initiative represents a critical step towards conserving biodiversity and protecting local ecosystems from invasive agricultural practices. Lambrechts understood the urgency of such measures, having witnessed first-hand how habitat destruction escalated over time. Engaging local communities became essential, fostering stewardship rather than exploitation.
