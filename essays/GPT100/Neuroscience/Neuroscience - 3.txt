When faced with new information or experiences, the individual may struggle to integrate these into their existing mental framework, further entrenching their cognitive distortions. This inability to adapt not only exacerbates depressive symptoms but also reinforces a cycle of despair, where the individual feels trapped in an unchanging reality. The generative model attributed to those with depression thus resembles an outdated blueprint, resistant to revisions that could pave the way for recovery or emotional resilience.
