Many people promote sustainability and ethical sourcing without grasping the economic pressures that make such ideals nearly impossible for smallholders. He emphasized that farms are not just businesses; they are part of a community fabric. This disconnect fosters an atmosphere of misunderstanding, where both sides harbor preconceptions shaped by their own experiences—urbanites romanticizing the rural lifestyle while farmers struggle under the weight of expectations and financial instability.
