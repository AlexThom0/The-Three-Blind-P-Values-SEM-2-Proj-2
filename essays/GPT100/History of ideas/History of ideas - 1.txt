This breadth of understanding not only allowed them to challenge conventional wisdom but also to draw connections between seemingly disparate ideas. In contrast, contemporary philosophers frequently find themselves constrained by disciplinary boundaries, leading to a fragmentation of thought. The reliance on technical jargon and niche arguments often alienates broader audiences, diminishing the potential impact of philosophical insights. By embracing a more interdisciplinary mindset, philosophers could strive to rekindle the spirit of inquiry that characterized earlier intellectual giants such as Nietzsche and Weber.
