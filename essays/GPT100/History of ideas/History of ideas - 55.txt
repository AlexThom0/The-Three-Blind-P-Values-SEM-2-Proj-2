This enigma compels us to search for overarching narratives that can lend coherence to the intricate tapestry of life. Such narratives often fall back on teleological language, as if to make sense of the chaos inherent in natural processes. Even in discussions surrounding evolution, we find ourselves slipping into explanations that imply intentionality. This tendency not only reflects our cognitive biases but also illuminates a deeper philosophical struggle: reconciling our understanding of a mechanistic universe with the human intuition that seeks purpose and meaning.
