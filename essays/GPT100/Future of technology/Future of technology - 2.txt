This tension led to the development of complex doctrines, such as Mutually Assured Destruction (MAD), which paradoxically created stability by ensuring that any offensive action would be met with catastrophic retaliation. Yet, this delicate balance could have easily tipped into chaos, emphasizing the fragility of human decision-making under stress. The question of whether technology shapes or merely reflects the motivations of those in power remains critical, as the potential for a catastrophic miscalculation looms large. Hence, understanding these dynamics is essential for navigating future technological advancements.
