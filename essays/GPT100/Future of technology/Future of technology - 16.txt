The festival embodies a radical rethinking of human connection to nature, urging participants to embrace a sense of humility in the face of impending collapse. By fostering spaces for storytelling and creativity, it seeks to cultivate resilience and an acknowledgment of our shared vulnerabilities. This is not merely a retreat into nostalgia but an active engagement with the present moment, urging individuals to confront the uncomfortable truths of modern existence. Through communal experiences, attendees are invited to envision a different future, one that insists on cooperation and collective vision as essential tools for navigating the turbulent times that lie ahead.
