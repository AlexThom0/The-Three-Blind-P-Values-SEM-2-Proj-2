This collaborative environment brought forth key figures like Nikola Tesla, whose alternating current system challenged Edison’s direct current dominance. As these various technologies evolved, so did the infrastructure necessary to support them. Factors such as regulatory developments and consumer adoption played critical roles in shaping the market.
