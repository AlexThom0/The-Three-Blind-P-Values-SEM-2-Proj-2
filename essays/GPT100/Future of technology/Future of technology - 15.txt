This experimental design revealed critical insights into how cognitive enhancers can impair social communication. While modafinil may enhance certain cognitive functions like attention and working memory, its impact on interpersonal skills became notably detrimental. Participants armed with powerful cognitive aids exhibited a tendency to overlook contextual cues, such as the need for collaborative dialogue. Their interactions shifted from cooperative exchanges to a more directive style of communication, underscoring the importance of social feedback in effective teamwork. Thus, the benefits of increased focus were countered by an erosion of essential interpersonal dynamics that facilitate collaborative problem-solving.
