These algorithms not only curate our experiences but also influence our preferences and perceptions, often creating echo chambers that reinforce certain viewpoints while marginalizing others. As the critic interacts with these dynamic networks, her interpretive strategies are shaped by the biases embedded in these technologies, further complicating our understanding of authorship and authority.
