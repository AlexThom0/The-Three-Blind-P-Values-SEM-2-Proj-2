The DRC became a platform to test innovations in robotics, particularly in scenarios paralleling the Fukushima crisis. Developers sought not only to enhance mobility and dexterity but also to instill a semblance of decision-making capabilities in machines. This ambition aimed at deploying robots in scenarios too perilous for humans, ultimately revealing the intersection of advanced technology and critical human safety. The stakes were high, as the lessons gleaned from Fukushima could shape future responses to disasters worldwide.
