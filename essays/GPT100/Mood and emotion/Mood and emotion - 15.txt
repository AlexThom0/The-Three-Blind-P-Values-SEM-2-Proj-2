When we encounter challenges, our feelings often reflect the degree of support we perceive from our social networks. A simple smile or nod can provide a boost to our confidence, allowing us to tackle tasks with renewed vigor. Conversely, feelings of isolation or criticism can exacerbate stress and diminish our sense of agency, making even small hurdles seem insurmountable. This intricate interplay underscores the importance of understanding cognitive appraisals in navigating everyday life.
