This cycle often results in a frustrating paradox, where the very activities meant to promote weight loss become mechanisms for rationalizing poor dietary decisions. As a consequence, people can inadvertently sabotage their efforts, feeling disillusioned and defeated. Understanding this complex interplay is crucial for redefining successful weight management strategies.
