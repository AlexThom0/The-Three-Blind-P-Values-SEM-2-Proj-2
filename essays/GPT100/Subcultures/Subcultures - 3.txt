The promotion of idealized bodies not only perpetuated unrealistic standards but also marginalized individuals who struggled to conform. Moreover, the emphasis on aesthetic appeal in nudism created a contradiction within its purported celebration of naturalism and freedom. As proponents highlighted the health benefits of nudism, they simultaneously established a rigid framework for acceptance that excluded diverse body types, ultimately transforming bodily liberation into a tool for social judgment rather than genuine inclusivity.
