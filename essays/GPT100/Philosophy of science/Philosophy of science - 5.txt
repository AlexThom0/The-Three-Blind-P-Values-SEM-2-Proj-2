Despite the advancements in understanding genetic mechanisms, the intricate orchestration required for tissue and organ development remains elusive. This complexity arises not merely from the molecules themselves but also from the dynamic interactions within cellular communities. These complex networks dictate how cells communicate, influence each other's destiny, and contribute to the overall architecture of a functioning organism. The challenge intensifies as cells must be guided not only to differentiate but also to organize spatially and temporally to form functional structures. Bridging this gap between molecular insights and holistic understanding is essential for unlocking true regenerative capabilities.
