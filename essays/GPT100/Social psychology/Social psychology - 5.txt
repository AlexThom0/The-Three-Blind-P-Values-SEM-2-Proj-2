The reactions following this exclusion revealed a stark contrast: those rejected displayed a marked decrease in emotional volatility but an increase in their willingness to engage in riskier behaviors. This dissonance highlights a complex emotional landscape where the drive for social connection can precipitate both aggression and diminished emotional responsiveness. Understanding this paradox may illuminate the psychological mechanisms at play, revealing how individuals navigate the challenging terrain of social belonging.
