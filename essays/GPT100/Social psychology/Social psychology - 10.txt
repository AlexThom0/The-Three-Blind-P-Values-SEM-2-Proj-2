This visceral aspect is not merely a backdrop but serves to illuminate deeper themes such as powerlessness, existential dread, and the fragility of human existence. By exposing our vulnerabilities, horror allows an exploration of the anxieties that linger beneath the surface of societal norms. The grotesque, rather than being an impediment to appreciation, acts as a gateway into understanding complex emotional landscapes. Thus, while the philosophical underpinnings of horror are essential, they should not overshadow the fundamental human truths explored through its more visceral elements.
