Confidence measures the certainty with which they make their assessments, while calibration indicates the degree to which their confidence reflects their true predictive ability. Individuals exhibiting high calibration are particularly valuable, as they possess the insight needed to discern when they should assert their knowledge and when to remain skeptical.
