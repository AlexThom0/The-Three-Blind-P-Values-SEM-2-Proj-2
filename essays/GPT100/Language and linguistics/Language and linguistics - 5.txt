The inscriptions not only serve as historical records but also provide insight into the cultural significance of these rituals. Each stela or temple tariff encapsulates a more profound narrative about identity and belonging within Carthaginian life. Moreover, the linguistic connections to other Semitic languages enrich our understanding of regional interactions and influences.
